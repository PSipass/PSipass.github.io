EnergyCostCalc

December 2024

Project Overview

EnergyCostCalc is a simple tool for regular people to calculate how much money they are losing on their usage of electronic household appliances such as coffee machines. In a time when economic hardship has become the norm, every outflow of money should be evaluated. With the tool, users can select specific models of common household appliances and calculate how much money they are spending in accordance with their usage frequency. Additionally, the tool also provides users with recommendations for more cost-effective appliances.

Made with: Aleksandr Petrov (co-creator) and Reyhan Danisan (supervisor)
