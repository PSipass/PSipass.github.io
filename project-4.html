The Untitled Hallway

January 2025

Project Overview

The Untitled Hallway is a digital audio experience in which the listener becomes a participant and takes an active role. Putting on a headset, the participant is transported to a massively long and wide hallway. A low droning sound emulates a fear of dread and any sound the participant makes is reverberated back to them with many layers of delay and echo effects. Should the participant stay quiet for too long, a random knocking sound or footsteps is heard, indicating the presence of some other something. Creating The Untitled Hallway was my first real attempt at making an experience focused on sound using the program PlugData.
