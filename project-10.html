SnackTime

June 2025

Project Overview

SnackTime is an interactive installation forcing a question upon anyone who interacts with it: “What is your time worth?”. The vending machine is filled with various snacks, drinks, and other items. However, users quickly find out that their normal form of currency, money, is useless. Each item has a price tag with a specific amount of time printed on it. A bag of chips is worth roughly two minutes, while a packet of cigarettes costs up to 45. To pay, users have to hold down a small button on each side of the vending machine, essentially hugging the machine for the duration specified on the price tag of the desired item. After completing the payment, the items falls out and a receipt is printed showing the user turned customer the details of their purchase.

Refunds not (physically) possible.

Made with: Mats Doornbos (co-creator), Timothy Schuur (co-creator), Fons Verbeek (coach)
