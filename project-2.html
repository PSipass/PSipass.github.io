HydreMine

January 2025

Project Overview

HydreMine is a two-player game where players control RC-cars to locate and dismantle mines hidden on a wooden arena. The mines take the form of hall effect sensors primed to react to differences in magnetic waves and the cars have strong neodymium magnets attached to them. By driving over designated areas, a tiny vibration motor affixed to the player’s body indicates whether they are parked above a live mine or not. If a vibration is felt, quickly pressing a button located next to the player dismantles the mine and awards them a point. The first player to three points wins the game and attains everlasting bragging rights (until the next game, of course).

Made with: Mats Doornbos, Timothy Schuur
