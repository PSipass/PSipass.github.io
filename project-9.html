Paradigm Shift Simulator

June 2025

Project Overview

Being tasked with creating something relating to the philosophy of science, I chose to make an interactive, visual representation of the different stages of a paradigm shift. With a central altar showcasing the current paradigm or ideology, the almost religious belief in the status quo is represented. By introducing a new ideology, however, the slow but steady rise is visible. After an eventual crisis stage the paradigm shift is successfully completed and a history of all paradigms so far is shown to the viewer. This process can be repeated endlessly.
