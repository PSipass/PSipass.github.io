Nervous Choir

April 2025

Project Overview

Nervous Choir is a collection of speakers with heart-shaped antennas all facing away from each other. Through a collection of beeps and boops, the speakers try to connect and make communication. Once achieved, they slowly but surely start to sing together. However, if a person gets too close to the nervous speakers, their voices begin to waver and eventually they even lose connection with each other. If left alone, they will find each other again, so for those who want to stop the music from ever being complete again, a pair of scissors is provided. Cutting the antenna of a speakers results in its death, forever leaving an uncomfortable blank.

Made with: Mats Doornbos
