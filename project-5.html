Wig Wush and Pat: The Tale of the Dream Hat

January 2025

Project Overview

Wig Wush and Pat: The Tale of the Dream Hat is a playful retelling of a research paper by Konkoly et al. (2021) about how two-way communication is possible even when one of the parties is lucid dreaming. In the Dr. Seuss inspired story, Wig Wush the Bug presents Pat the Bear with a ‘Dream Hat’, allowing them to continue talking to each other even while Pat is dreaming. It mentions how, just like in the research paper, Pat can twitch his eyebrows to answer any questions he hears from Wig Wush. The questions Wig Wush asks almost seamlessly appear in Pat’s dream as voices from the sky.
